Basecamp.

Basecamp is a creative label aimed at innovating hip hop culture. We heavily inspire from Indonesian pop culture in our projects and We host our parties at several nightlife hostpots around Groningen. Be sure to follow us on social media to stay updated about our latest events, merchandise, and projects!

As a music collective, our vision is to share our passion of music with our audience to our full capabilities. We aim to add some diversity into Groningen's nightlife. Being comprised of mainly international students, we believe that our combined creative knowledge would provide a wide variety of innovative vibes to the parties in the city. While also staying up to date with the newest hits, we never neglect old school songs and try to shake up the atmosphere with some timeless classics and all time favourites.
