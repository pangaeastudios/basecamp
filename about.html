Welcome to the party!

Northsound is a student run music collective based in Groningen. Our vision as a music collective is to share our passion of music with our audience to our full capabilities. With genres ranging from Techno to Hip-hop, we aim to capture the full party experience. We host our parties at several nightlife hostpots around Groningen. So be sure to follow us on social media to stay updated about our latest events and merchandise!

As a collective, we aim to add some diversity to Groningen's nightlife. Being comprised of mainly International students, we believe that our combined music knowledge would provide a wide variety of vibes to the parties in the city. While also staying up to date with the newest hits, we never neglect old school songs and try to shake up the atmosphere with some timeless classics and all time favourites.
